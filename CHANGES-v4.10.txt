FIXES IN v4.10

1. Clean engine replacement
   The old v3.02 deterministic scout/shared-SL engine is not used.

2. Individual protection
   Every pending order is submitted with its own SL and TP. Filled positions restore missing protection, move toward break-even, and trail independently.

3. No premature direction flip
   An active campaign cannot reverse. After flat, a quiet reset is mandatory. The opposite direction needs a stronger score held for longer.

4. Broker-valid pending orders
   Pending stops are GTC only. No expiry timestamp is sent.

5. Safer cancellation
   Orders are reselected before deletion, freeze-zone cancellations are deferred, and a fill during cancellation is accepted and protected instead of treated as a fatal state error.

6. Railway stability
   Heartbeat and command polling are rate-limited. Basket reports are low-frequency. HTTP failures use exponential backoff instead of flooding Railway.
