EVE FURY RECONSTRUCTION DEMO v4.00

1. Deploy the railway folder to the existing Railway service only if you want the dashboard.
2. In MetaEditor open mt5/EVE_Fury_Reconstruction_Demo_v4.00.mq5.
3. Press Compile. The source was statically checked here but could not be compiled in MetaEditor in this environment.
4. Attach it to XAUUSD M1 on a HEDGING demo account.
5. Enable Algo Trading and allow the Railway WebRequest URL if using the dashboard.
6. Keep lot size at 0.01 and Maximum Positions at 10 for the first demo run.

DO NOT USE ON A LIVE ACCOUNT.
