EVE MOMENTUM BURST v2.01 — FULL REPLACEMENT

This ZIP replaces only the EVE-MOMENTUM-BURST repository.
It does not change EVE Gold Runner or EVESBOT.

1. Confirm the old Momentum Burst EA has no open positions or pending orders.
2. Disable autonomous mode and remove v1.10 from the XAUUSD M1 chart.
3. Delete the old repository contents and upload everything inside this folder.
4. Railway Root Directory remains: railway
5. Keep these Railway variables:
   BOT_TOKEN=EVE-MOMENTUM-DEMO-2026
   AUTO_ENABLED=true
   DATA_DIR=/data
6. Wait for Railway to show v2.0.1 online.
7. Open mt5/EVE_Momentum_Burst_EA_v2.01.mq5 in MetaEditor and press F7.
8. Attach the compiled EA to XAUUSD M1.
9. EA Inputs:
   InpRailwayBaseUrl=https://eve-momentum-burst-production.up.railway.app
   InpBotToken=EVE-MOMENTUM-DEMO-2026
10. MT5 Tools > Options > Expert Advisors must contain the same https URL.
11. Open the Railway dashboard and apply the lot/ladder settings you want.

Default dashboard settings:
- Fixed lot: 0.01
- Initial positions: 1
- Maximum positions: 20
- Maximum total lots: 0.20
- Balance scaling: OFF

The dashboard settings affect new positions only. Existing positions are never resized.
