## Decisive finding

The single scout position was profitable as a group. Every completed multi-position basket lost.

Only four closed positions carried the `EVE31-CONF` comment, and no closed position carried `EVE31-LAD`. The bot therefore was not losing because a mature ladder was giving back profit. It was losing because the second/opposite stage was reached without enough proof and turned otherwise manageable scout attempts into larger losing baskets.

## Design response in v3.01

- One directional scout stop rather than a two-sided live bracket.
- No opposite reversal or hedge inside a campaign.
- Dynamic protection and momentum-stall banking for the scout.
- Position 2 delayed until the scout has earned and retained profit, secured a broker-side lock, and price re-accelerates.
- Only one future ladder order, requiring a profitable basket and a fresh continuation burst.
- Dashboard performance filtered to the current v3.01 magic so older bot records cannot contaminate the new results.
